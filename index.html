Welcome to The Highlands

 

We'd like to give you a bit of information about our exciting new residential development located in the southwest corner of Olds, Alberta. We have chosen to name our streets after men from the Olds community who became casualties of World War II, as a memorial to them and a reminder to us all to value our freedom.

Some of the features which will be offered in this spectacular new development are as follows:

Underground electrical services

Wide multi-use sidewalks

Paved trails

Freedom of choice in design and builder

Proposed community garden

Proposed dedicated bicycle lane on main streets

Wide choice of multi-zoned lots
